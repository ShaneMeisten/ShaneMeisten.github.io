About this Project:

Through out the years of gaming the same few questions always seem to be asked "Which is the best console", "Which is the best game", "Who is the best developer". This projects is going to answer those questions and more.

All of these visualizations are based on data up until 2018 as those had the most sales, and will look at total sales of each of the following: By Year, By Genre, By Console, By Developer and By Critic Score.

The data used for this project is from Maven where it is the first option

And all sales are calculate per million. (e.g. If you see a 1 in total sales then it is 1,000,000)
