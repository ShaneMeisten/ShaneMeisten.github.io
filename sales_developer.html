Sales by Developer

Developers are the people who control the space. The games these developers make have a huge impact on gaming due to the popularity of themselves. Having that much popularity can be a double edge sword because if their games is good then the publicity gained will help, but at the same time if the game is bad then their popularity comes back to bite them. Example will be the Call of Duty series, the franchise is massively popular and with that comes a lot of sales and you see that with the 3 developers of the franchise all making into the top 20 sold games.
